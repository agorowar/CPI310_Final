About Us

Paw Me Maybe is a social media site for pet owners. Pet owners can find other pet owners and find pets that private owners are wanting to adopt out.
Users have the ability to create their own Pet Owner Profile. Users can upload their own information, for example the city the user is based in,
images of their pets, and the personality and traits of their pets. Owners can find and match with other pets/pet owners to chat and set up playdates.
Users can also match pets with new pet owners to set up adoption.
